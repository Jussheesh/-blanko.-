ABOUT BLANKO.

Our Mission

blanko. was born from a simple frustration: why is custom merch in the Philippines so expensive and complicated? We built a platform that treats every user as an artist — giving them the blank, the tools, and the press — without the international shipping fees and bloated software costs.

Inspired by the freedom of designing your own Minecraft skin, blanko. brings that same joyful ownership to real-world merchandise. You design it, we make it, you wear it.

Local Sourcing

Every garment we use is sourced locally in the Philippines. Our printing partners are based right here in Cebu, which means faster turnaround, lower costs, and real accountability. No middlemen, no mystery.

We use quality blank garments — the same type used by independent clothing brands — and print using DTG (Direct-to-Garment) and screen printing depending on your design's needs.
